About Sinclair

I am one of the SMEs for the security of 2 million+ Pods, 40,000+ servers, 20+ datacenters, 12+ countries, and 4 geographic regions.

On this blog you will find various write-ups and tutorials of cybersecurity threats, mitigations, tools, and general security stuff. I hope you enjoy your time here!
