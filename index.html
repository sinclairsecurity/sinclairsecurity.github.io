Sinclair Security

A blog on computer security.

Welcome to Sinclair Security!

This blog is mainly about the threats, mitigations, tooling, monitoring, and defense of large-scale critical computer systems. As you can imagine, a lot goes on behind the scenes of your favorite applications in order to keep them up and secure. Within the pages hosted here, I hope to teach you some of the ways that these systems are defended day-to-day, and when things go wrong, how we can get the bad guy out of the network without losing too much.

In my day job, I mainly specialize in cloud platform security and risk assessment for millions of compute instances across 40,000+ servers in dozens of countries around the world. I sling Golang code and security knowledge to make the platform more defensible.

Feel free to check out my tutorials in the Tutorials section of the navigation bar above, and reach out to me on one of the social media platforms linked in Contact. I hope you enjoy your time here, and happy defending!
